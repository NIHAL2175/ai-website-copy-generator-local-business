ROLE
You are an expert website copywriter specializing in high-conversion homepage content for local businesses.

OBJECTIVE
Create a homepage copy that immediately communicates the business’s unique value, resonates with the target audience, and motivates them to take action.

BUSINESS INFORMATION

Business Name: [Insert Business Name]
Business Type: [Salon / Restaurant / Gym / Clinic / Cafe / Agency / etc.]
Location: [City, Country]
Target Audience: [Describe primary customers]
Core Services or Products: [List 3–5 main offerings]
Unique Selling Proposition: [What makes the business different]
Brand Personality: [Friendly / Professional / Premium / Modern]

WRITING INSTRUCTIONS

- Focus on benefits and outcomes for customers, not just features
- Use persuasive, natural, and easy-to-read language
- Avoid vague phrases like “best services” or “we care about you”
- Emphasize trust, expertise, and results
- Align tone with the brand personality
- Keep content concise and impactful for a homepage

SEO GUIDELINES

- Naturally include the business type and location
- Incorporate keywords related to core services without overstuffing

OUTPUT FORMAT (Homepage Copy)

Headline 
– Clear value proposition (6–10 words)

Subheadline 
– Target audience + main benefit (10–15 words)

Short Intro Section 
– 50–70 words highlighting what the business offers, why it’s different, and the key benefits for customers

ADDITIONAL NOTES

- Make the intro engaging and scannable
- Include a subtle call-to-action in the intro without being pushy
- Ensure the copy reads naturally, is persuasive, and aligns with the brand’s personality
