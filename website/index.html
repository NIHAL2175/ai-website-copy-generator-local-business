GOT QUESTIONS?

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

WHAT ARE THE GYM TIMINGS?
Our gym is open daily from 5:30 AM to 10:00 PM, including weekends. You can choose early morning, evening, or late evening slots based on your routine.
DO YOU OFFER A FREE TRIAL SESSION?
Yes. We offer one free trial session so you can experience the gym, meet a trainer, and understand which plan fits your goals before joining.
I'M A COMPLETE BEGINNER — CAN I STILL JOIN?
Absolutely. Beginners are welcome. Our coaches start with your current fitness level and build a step-by-step plan focused on safe form and steady progress.
WHAT MEMBERSHIP PLANS DO YOU HAVE?
We offer Starter, Pro, and Elite plans. Starter covers gym access, Pro adds trainer support and diet guidance, and Elite includes premium one-on-one coaching.
IS PERSONAL TRAINING INCLUDED IN MY MEMBERSHIP?
Personal training is included in Pro (limited sessions) and Elite (extended support). If you are on Starter, you can add personal training as an upgrade.
DO YOU PROVIDE DIET OR NUTRITION GUIDANCE?
Yes. We provide practical nutrition guidance based on your goal, whether it is fat loss, muscle gain, or body recomposition, with easy meal structure tips.
